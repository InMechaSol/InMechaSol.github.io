InMechaSol's XPlat Architecture

A Computing Architecture for Mechatronic Products

InMechaSol's XPlat Architecture for OEM Systems is an open source solution for product manufactures, scientist, engineers, and students.

It is designed to:

  • maximize reuse of core intellectual property.
  • promote innovation over recreation.
  • promote robust reliable products with integrated computing systems.
  • reduce time required by product developments.
  • improve production, quality, and service with enterprise tools.
  • simplify compliance with regulatory agencies.
