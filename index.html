InMechaSol - It Can be Done!

InMechaSol has the non profit mission to advance industry and education through mechatronic innovation.

InMechaSol's Training Center and Open Source Solutions are intended for product manufactures, scientist, engineers, and students.

InMechaSol's work:

  • creates synergies within manufacturing organizations.
  • promotes the development of robust reliable solutions.
  • promotes new innovation over recreation, reuse don't remake.
  • simplifies complex development tasks.
  • simplifies compliance with regulatory agencies.
  • helps students "Bridge the Gap" from education to implementation.
